Language Selector

Language Selector
Once your business has gone international, you will quickly need to think about customizing your site for different languages.

Language Selector

English
  • dansk
  • English

Sitecore as a leading Content Management System provides an ability to create multiple language versions for each of the items. In Sitecore Experience Accelerator, we fully support this. What is more, we have created a component named Language Selector which enables to easily and quickly switch between available languages.

Take a look below to see how Language Selector works!

With Site and Language Selectors it is so easy now to make use of multilingual sites!
